About Us

Crafting Automotive Excellence Since 2023

At Luxury Tint & Wrap, we're passionate about transforming vehicles into works of art. Our team of expert technicians combines years of experience with the latest technology to deliver flawless finishes that exceed expectations.

Whether you're looking for sleek, dark tints for added privacy and UV protection or a custom-designed wrap to make your car stand out, we have you covered. We use only premium materials to ensure your car not only looks stunning but stays protected from the elements.

Our attention to detail and commitment to quality has made us the preferred choice for automotive enthusiasts throughout Van Nuys and the surrounding areas.
